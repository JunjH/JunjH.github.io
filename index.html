Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor - Presidential Young Fellow at the School of Artificial Intelligence, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. Previously, I was a researcher at the Shenzhen Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics for Society from 2020.04-2025.07. I got my Ph.D. in the Graduate School of Information Sciences, Tohoku University, supervised by Prof. Takayuki Okatani in 2020.

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D., M.Phil., and research assistant/intern candidates. If you are interested, please feel free to contact me via email.

Research Interests

  • Embodied AI: Robot Navigation, Manipulation, Multi-Sensor Fusion, Multi-robot Collaboration
  • Visual Perception: 3D Vision, SLAM, Monocular Depth Estimation, Depth Completion
  • Machine Learning: Lifelong Learning, Knowledge Distillation, Self/Semi-Supervised Learning
