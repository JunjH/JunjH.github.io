Biography

I am currently a researcher at the Shenzhen Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics for Society in Shenzhen, working with Prof. Tin Lun Lam. I got my Ph.D in the Graduate School of Information Sciences, Tohoku University, supervised by Prof. Takayuki Okatani in 2020. My research interest lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics, mainly including lifelong learning, 3D vision, robot perception, SLAM, multi-robot perception.

Please contact me if you are interested in an internship or a research assistant position.
